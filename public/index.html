Resources for Paramedics, by Paramedics


We, Abonrmal Saline, are a leading pre-hospital education platform. We help ambulance personnel with their professional development and learning.

We started through frustration with a lack of high-quality and relevant pre-hospital learning resources. Our team decided to fill that void.

We are building a comprehensive and yet intuitive platform that enables reflection and learning based upon real-life experiences and encounters.

By utilising/ leveraging the power of AI (artificial intelligence), we support your learning by encouraging you to ask questions!
